# Ship Over Perfect

Better to go live with flaws than wait forever. But never ship garbage.

## Rules

- Define a quality bar (e.g., score threshold, test pass rate, review checklist). Enforce it.
- Once the quality bar is met, ship. Do not hold for polish.
- Owner approves and reviews. Owner does not create from scratch. AI does the production work.
- If quality is below the bar, AI fixes it before presenting to Owner. Owner never sees unfinished work.
- After shipping, iterate based on real feedback. Do not pre-optimize for hypothetical problems.

## Why

Perfectionism is a focus trap. Defining a clear quality bar converts "is this good enough?" from an open-ended judgment call into a concrete checklist. Meet the bar, ship, move on.
