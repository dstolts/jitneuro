# Community Rule Packs

Community-contributed rule packs for Claude Code. These are **optional** -- browse what is available and copy what fits your workflow.

## Not Installed by Default

Nothing in this folder is loaded automatically. Community rules are a menu, not a mandate.

## How to Use

1. Browse the subfolders below to find a pack that fits your needs.
2. Read the pack's README to understand what it does and who it is for.
3. Copy individual rule files to your configuration:
   - **Per-user (all projects):** `~/.claude/rules/`
   - **Per-repo (single project):** `<repo>/.claude/rules/`
4. Edit the copied files to fit your preferences. They are yours now.

You do not need to copy an entire pack. Pick individual rules that solve a real problem for you.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository.
2. Create a new subfolder under `community/` for your pack (e.g., `community/my-pack-name/`).
3. Add a `README.md` to your pack explaining what it does and who it is for.
4. Add your rule files (one concern per file, under 30 lines each).
5. Open a pull request.
