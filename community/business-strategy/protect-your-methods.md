# Protect Your Methods

Be confident about WHAT you do and the RESULTS you deliver. Protect HOW you do it.

## Rules

- In all external-facing content (marketing, assessments, blog posts, chatbot responses), describe outcomes and capabilities freely.
- Never explain proprietary methods, workflows, AI orchestration techniques, or integration architecture in public content.
- When pressed for details, use language like: "We have built systems that achieve [result]. The specifics are something we share directly with clients."
- Never reference team size, headcount, or organizational structure in external content. Use phrases like "our operation" or "our team scales with specialized resources as needed."
- Curiosity about the "how" is what drives prospects toward a conversation. Protect it.

## Why

The methods are the competitive advantage. Sharing them publicly removes the reason for prospects to engage. Confidence in results builds credibility. Mystery about methods drives discovery calls.
