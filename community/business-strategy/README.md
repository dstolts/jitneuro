# Business Strategy Rule Pack

Rules for AI-assisted business operations. These help Claude Code behave appropriately in a business context -- protecting proprietary methods, framing external communication correctly, and keeping AI output aligned with business goals.

## Rules

| Rule | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| protect-your-methods.md | Be confident about results. Protect how you achieve them. |

## Who This Is For

Business owners, consultants, and agencies using Claude Code for client-facing work, marketing, or operations where competitive advantage matters.

## How to Use

Copy individual files to `~/.claude/rules/` (global) or `<repo>/.claude/rules/` (per-project). Edit to taste.
